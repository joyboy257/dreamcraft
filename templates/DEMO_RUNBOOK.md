## Recovery branches

### Generation slow

Continue narration until timeout; app enters stable fragment. Then show cached primary dream.

### API/network failure

Acknowledge the fallback as intentional reliability behavior; load cached contrasting dream.

### Pointer lock failure

Click canvas/Enter Dream control again; use documented fallback controls if available.

### Frame-rate drop

Switch to balanced/reduced quality; avoid debug overlay in final recording.
